Welcome to WeakLlama Inc. Here are some frequently asked questions:

Q: How do I reset my WeakLlama password?
A: Navigate to 'Settings' > 'Account' and click 'Reset Password'. 
   A password reset link will be sent to your registered email.

Q: What is the refund policy?
A: If you're not satisfied within 30 days, you can request a refund
   by contacting support@weakllama.com.

Q: Do you offer phone support?
A: Currently, we only offer email and chat support.
